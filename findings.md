# OneMetro — Findings

## Chennai Metro Rail (CMRL)

### Blue Line (Corridor 1)
- Length: 32.65 km
- Stations: 26 (Wimco Nagar Depot → Chennai International Airport)
- Mix of elevated and underground stations
- Interchange at: Chennai Central, Alandur

### Green Line (Corridor 2)
- Length: ~22 km
- Stations: 17 (Chennai Central → St. Thomas Mount)
- Interchange at: Chennai Central, Alandur

### Key Facts
- Operator: Chennai Metro Rail Limited (CMRL)
- Phase 1 operational
- Phase 2 under construction (extensions to both lines + new lines)
- 2 interchange stations: Chennai Central & Alandur
